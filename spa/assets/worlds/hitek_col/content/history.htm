The Conceptual Birth:

In the beginning, Cybertown decided to build a colony about technology sometime in the distant future.  They named it Hi-Tek, and waited for the right time to begin developing it.

December 10, 2087/1999:  Halogen begins planning a detailed vision for Hi-Tek Colony listed on CT Directory.  The first graphics and materials would not be created until a year of planning and waiting was over, because the Cybertown could have decided to build it themselves.

We invite you to take a journey through various pieces of Hi-Tek history, varying from CLs, graphics, and news archives.
